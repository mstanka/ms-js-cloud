Moving to Cloud

my personal perspective after consulting with 100+ companies

Cloud is everywhere today. People use it even if they don’t realize it. There are over 2 billion active users on Facebook, over 1 billion active users on Instagram, 300 million users on Amazon. And so on.

On the other hand, a lot of companies I work with are cautious of cloud computing. They even think they don’t need it. I advise to all sorts of companies — local, global, multinational, national, private, public. Slowly some of them start to consider using cloud platforms for their minor workloads.
